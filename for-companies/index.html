N° 06 Questions, before the call
Where does the audio live? +

Wherever you want it to. Private RSS for a podcast app, files for SharePoint or intranet, or a feed your LMS can pull. We don't host anything publicly and we don't keep copies after delivery — beyond what your contract permits.

Who owns the recordings and the IP? +

You. Full rights to the audio, the transcripts, the format. NDAs are standard. We can sign yours; we have one of our own if you'd rather use it.

What languages do you work in? +

Dutch and English. Most internal programmes run in the language people actually speak at work — we'll match that, not the language that looks more polished on slides.

Do we need a recording room? +

A quiet meeting room is enough. We bring the gear, set up the acoustics, and break down before the next meeting. One hour per episode, including setup.

How does procurement / invoicing work? +

We invoice per phase or per episode block, with standard 30-day terms. Belgian VAT registered. We can work against a PO and fit your supplier intake process.
