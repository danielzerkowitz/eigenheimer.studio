N° 07 Questions, before the call
How much of my time does this actually take? +

One hour per episode for the recording itself. About 90 minutes upfront for the intake and format conversations. Roughly 15 minutes per episode to approve the cut and the derived posts. Everything else is on us.

Who owns the show, the audio, and the posts? +

You. Full rights on the audio, the show, the format, the derived assets. You can take any of it elsewhere at any time. We keep a portfolio mention with your written permission — that's the only thing on our side.

Can we record in Dutch? +

Yes. We work in Dutch and English. Many partners record in Dutch because their buyers are Dutch-speaking. We'll match the language to the audience you actually sell to, not the one that feels more international.

How much editorial control do I have? +

Final cut is yours. We propose the format, structure the conversation, edit the audio, and write the post drafts. Nothing publishes without your sign-off. We are editors, not ghostwriters — your voice stays your voice.

Why only four active partners at a time? +

Because editorial brain doesn't scale. The whole point of the cascade is that one person actually thinks about your show every week. Four is the number where we can still do that. If the slate is full, we'll tell you a realistic start date.
